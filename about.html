About Me

I'm an innovative thinker and problem solver with several years of experience in a variety of roles including financial analysis, technical and customer support, leadership, project management, and most recently both client account management and full stack web development.

I posses an excellent and distinguishable work ethic, allowing me to consistently deliver exceptional results. I am a great team player with a keen ability to identify people's strenghts and find the best ways to collaborate. I have a genuine interest in solving complex problems, taking great pride in the research and development process, and ultimately finding a viable solution. Furthermore, I am able to do this while considering team dynamics, company and project objectives, as well as client expectations.

I'm currently a Transactions Cost Analysis Account Manager at Bloomberg LP, where I am leveraging technical and client facing skills from previous roles. My main responsibility is to assist clients in optimizing their usage of the platform, while creating and identifying opportunities for additional revenue. In addition to this, I also collaborate with product managers and engineers to discuss product feedback, and formulate ideas on how to improve the product. Prior to this, I held several technical, analytical and client support roles at Bloomberg LP. These include: Integrations Project Manager for the TCA product, Escalated Support Specialist for the TCA product, and Support Specialist for the Equity Analytics platform.

I'm always looking for ways to grow professionally and personally, which led me to pursue Full Stack Web Development through Columbia University Engineering's coding bootcamp. I hope to combine my newly acquired software development skills with my previous career experiences in order to become an innovative software engineer in the areas of machine learning and artificial intelligence.
